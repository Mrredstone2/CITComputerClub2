Computer Club President

Hello. My name is Sid Kumar, and I am the president of Computer Club for the 2021-22 school year. Computer Club is a place where people who are interested in computer related topics can gather and learn more about those topics amongst like-minded people. The club is split into groups that each have a focus on a different area of computer science, where each week the group lead has a lesson on a topic in that interest area.

Videogame Dev

Our goal for the video game design section of computer club is to work together and create a game while exploring the various aspects of game development. To achieve this, we have divided our section into 3 groups: the game designers, script writers, and asset creators. Through the different groups available, students will have the opportunity to explore all aspects of game development and choose one that they like the most.

Cybersecurity

Welcome to Computer Club. My name is Carson Krahe and I am the group leader for cybersecurity. So, what do we do here? We do CyberPatriot, which is a nationwide competition where we find vulnerabilities in virtual computer systems. Now that sounds like a lot, but it is basically a scavenger hunt to find ways to make your system more secure. So, some questions you may have: Do I need any experience? No, you don’t need any experience and I will help you up to the first competition and you will have teammates to help you. What will you be learning about?
